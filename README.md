# 🎧 Audio EQ & Visualizer

A lightweight Chrome extension to sculpt your sound experience in real-time on any tab.

## 🚀 Installation (Developer Mode)

1. Download the extension files into a folder.
2. Open Chrome and go to `chrome://extensions/`.
3. Enable **"Developer mode"** (top right).
4. Click **"Load unpacked"** and select your folder.
5. Pin the extension for quick access.

---

## 🛠 Features

### 🎛 3-Band Equalizer
* **BASS / MID / TREBLE:** Fine-tune frequencies with precise vertical sliders.
* **RST Buttons:** Quick reset for each frequency band.

### 📈 Dynamic Frequency Curve
A real-time visualization canvas showing your current EQ shape.

### 🔊 Volume Control (Gain Boost)
* **0% to 200%:** Adjust the tab volume, including a 2x boost for quiet content.

### ⚡ Quick Functions
* **LOUDNESS:** One-click +6dB boost to bass and treble for richer low-volume listening.
* **BYPASS:** Instantly toggle between your custom EQ and the original sound.
* **START/STOP EQ:** Manage tab audio capture easily.

---

## ⚠️ Notes
* **Refresh:** Always refresh your audio tab (e.g., YouTube) after an update for the capture to work.
* **Privacy:** All audio is processed locally; no data leaves your browser.
